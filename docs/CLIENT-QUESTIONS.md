# Open Questions

Some questions are for you, and some can later be asked to your grandpa or early users.

1. Where should the app be created long-term: directly in this repo root, or inside a `dashboard/` subfolder?
2. News scope: which sources, RSS only or specific publishers, keyword filtering or all items?
3. Calendar scope: primary calendar only, or all calendars including shared?
4. Drive scope: recent files globally, a chosen folder, or files selected by the user?
5. Multi-account support? MVP should ideally be one signed-in Google account at a time.
6. Long-term hosting: custom domain, or a `.pages.dev` URL?
7. Should dashboard layout/settings sync across devices? MVP should be per-device local settings.
8. Visual style: calm/simple family dashboard, power-user dashboard, or something more playful?
